FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to pay for the AI?
No — MeetBrief uses your own API key (Anthropic Claude or Ollama). The license fee is for the app only. AI costs are billed directly to you by the provider, typically a few cents per meeting.
What happens after the 25-day trial?
The app stops processing new transcriptions until you enter a license key. Your data and settings are preserved. Purchase a Personal license at any time to continue without interruption.
Is the source code available?
Yes — the core is open source on GitHub. The Personal and Enterprise licenses cover the compiled Electron app with auto-updates, support, and premium features not in the public repo.
Can I switch plans?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel any time. Unused days are prorated on annual plans. Switching from monthly to annual saves 30% and takes effect on your next billing date.
