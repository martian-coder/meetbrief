Capabilities

Everything you need to
never be caught off guard

Built for people who want to stay fully present in conversations while having an AI quietly ready with the right words.

Real-time transcription — hands free

Whisper runs locally, capturing system audio and transcribing each turn with no cloud API call. A rolling 30-second window is maintained automatically.

AI replies streamed instantly

When the speaker pauses, the LLM fires. Responses stream token-by-token so the first words appear in under a second. No waiting — just glancing.

Transparent always-on-top overlay

A frameless, click-through window floats above every app. Toggle visibility or click-through mode with a hotkey while you speak.

Workflow

Three steps from context to
confident reply

1

Load your context

Paste your agenda, role, and objectives before the meeting. This becomes the cached system prompt — the AI knows the full picture before anyone speaks.

2

Start listening

Click Start. MeetBrief captures system audio, runs Silero VAD to detect speech boundaries, and transcribes each turn locally with Whisper.

3

Reply with confidence

When they pause, the LLM fires. A suggested reply streams to the overlay in under two seconds. Glance, adapt, and say it. Or use the hotkey for on-demand generation.
